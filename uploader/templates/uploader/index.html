Welcome

Why another teaching resource site? We all know that teachers are re-inventing the wheel with resources despite the fact there's lots of great resources out there.

The biggest problem is that resources that are aimed at specific courses are harder to find than generic ones. A resource teaching fractions to 11 year olds is going to be different from those for 16 year olds. openTeach's aim is to try and collect the best resources for each exam syllabus rather than try to collect as many resources as possible.

> Over time the site will become smarter, similiar topics will be linked and resources will be shared between exam boards where appropriate.
